Printed Media

This is a hand bound, french fold book, designed and bound by Chianti Dixon.

This book is a compilation of three TED talks given by Roman Mars, Tony Fadell, and Chip Kidd. These designers focused on one word, noticing. The cover of the book is an item that gets overlooked everyday, we use it so often that we don’t even notice it is there. Everyday people don’t notice the design of products they use, and as designers we have to look look closer and to think bigger.
